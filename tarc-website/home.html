Our Mission

Phoenix Rocketry hopes to promote the growth of rocketry in the Pacific Northwest by supporting, promoting, and initiating activities such as summer camps, participating in The American Rocketry Challenge, and working with the National Association of Rocketry. We hope to provide both theoretical education combined with an executional framework.
